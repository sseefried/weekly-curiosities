Meditation can be bad for you?

Have you ever heard that meditation could, under certain circumstances, be harmful? I certainly hadn't. It was such an interesting view that I just had to read David Chapman's take on it. Now, let's be clear, he doesn't say it's always harmful or even mostly harmful just that it _can_ be harmful. The basic gist is that _depersonalisation_ can result from practice of intentionally trying to annihilate the self in Mindfulness.

Why is the night sky not just one big white sheet?

It's not immediately obvious why the entire night sky is not just one big white sheet. If we live in an infinite universe with infinite stars - the consensus view - at least some of the light from even far away stars is going to reach us. Also the further away a star is the more stars there are at that radius, so that should mean more light. So why the big, dark gaps in between stars in the night sky?
